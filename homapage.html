Too late to notice

Shortfilm - Sonder

Sonder

Every day, we walk past hundreds of people. On the street, in class, on the bus. Most of the time, we don’t really see them. They are just part of the background of our own lives. But each of these people has their own story, their own thoughts, fears, memories, and dreams.

The idea of Sonder is about realizing this. It is the moment when you understand that everyone around you is living a life just as real and complex as your own. This realization can feel small, but it can also change the way we see the world and the people in it.

Our short film “Too late to notice” explores what happens when we fail to see others in time. It shows how easy it is to ignore people, even when they are right in front of us. Often, we only understand their importance when the moment has already passed. (anpassen)

With this film, we want to slow things down and invite the audience to look closer. To notice the people we usually overlook. And to ask ourselves what might change if we started paying attention earlier - before it is too late.
