ACT 2 — THE CHASE

The Pursuit of a Fleeting Truth

There is a sudden, sharp hunger that habit can no longer satisfy. It is the moment you realize that time is leaking through your fingers like neon light. The chase is not a run toward a destination, but a desperate sprint to catch up to a version of yourself that is still truly alive. Between the blur of passing lights and the echo of footsteps, we hunt for a glitch in the system—a sign, a glance, or a memory that proves we are more than just a ghost in the machinery of the night.
