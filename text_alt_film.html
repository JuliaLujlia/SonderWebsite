General description

This short film portrays a man in his thirties who is trapped in a monotonous urban everyday life. Through repetition, visual isolation, and inner monologues, the film explores emotional numbness, alienation from other people, and the consequences of living on autopilot. A life-changing encounter with a stranger forces the man to slow down, reflect, and reconnect with the people and world around him.

The story is told through a combination of images, ambient city sounds, voice-over narration, and sparse dialogue. Lighting, framing, and editing rhythm play a central role in conveying mood and meaning.

Characters

The man (30s):
Dressed in a corporate coat. He is handsome but looks drained. He exists in a self-imposed bubble of isolation.
The stranger (40s-50s):
Homeless. Wears layers. He is not a villain; he is a watcher.
