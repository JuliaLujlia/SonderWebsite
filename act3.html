ACT 3 — THE AWAKENING

The Infinite Tapestry of Souls

The veil finally thins, and the gravity of the 'Other' collapses upon you. This is the awakening: the profound and overwhelming realization of Sonder. You look at a stranger and see a universe—a library of unread books, a lifetime of secrets, and a heart that beats with the same intensity as your own. In this moment, you are no longer the protagonist of the world, but a single thread in a vast, invisible tapestry. The city is no longer a backdrop of concrete; it is a living, breathing symphony of millions of stories happening all at once.
