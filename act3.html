The man walks through a busy avenue at sunset with benches. He is walking towards the camera.

Voice-over (the man): I wonder where he is now. Is he sleeping on that bench?

Voice-over (the man): Now… I can’t stop imagining his life. Maybe he had a job like mine once? Maybe...

Voice-over (the man): I see the surface. But underneath…

Path further above the river. A couple moves toward the camera.

The Connection Beneath the Surface

Once you start wondering, you cannot stop. You see the surface, but underneath, there is a whole ocean you will never truly see. Everyone is busy with their own script, their own panic, their own desire. But life is short, and it is wide—you just have to be brave enough to press pause.
